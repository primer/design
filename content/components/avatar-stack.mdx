## Usage

Stacked avatars can be used to show multiple collaborators or participants when there is limited space available. When you hover over the stack, the avatars will reveal themselves.

### Best practices

- Use AvatarStack when space is limited
- Donâ€™t use AvatarStack if there is adequate space to show 4 avatars or less.

## Anatomy

Avatar stack displays a minimum of 2 avatars and a maximum of 4 avatars.
Only the 20px avatar size is currently used in the avatar stack.

## Options

### Alignment

The default AvatarStack is left-aligned. You can [right-align](https://primer.style/react/AvatarStack#right-aligned) the component for layouts that are better suited for it.
